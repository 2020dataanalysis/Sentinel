Help Page

Activate Alarm

Login to activate or upon closing time the alarm will automatically activate.

Deactivate Alarm

At any time, login to deactivate or use the bluetooth key.
During closed hours, the alarm will stay deactivated only if the bluetooth key is on and in range. Otherwise, the alarm will automatically reactivate itself.
During open hours, once the alarm is deactivated it will stay deactivated. (Regardless of the presence of a bluetooth key.)
